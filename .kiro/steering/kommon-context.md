# Kommon: University-Verified Student Housing Platform (NYC)

**Core Mission**: Trusted, closed-ecosystem marketplace for students. Mandatory .edu verification eliminates scams.

**Target Users**: International grad students, out-of-state undergrads, local NYC students seeking roommates

**Key Features**: .edu verification, dual marketplace (need/have room), lifestyle compatibility matching

**Current Phase**: 2A - Room listings (Phase 1A-1C complete: landing, auth, profiles)
